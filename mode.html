Meet

Revolutionizing virtual meetings

Experience the ultimate conferencing solution with complete sync, echo-free communication, noise cancellation, and the option to integrate with popular conferencing applications like Zoom. Also, dial cellular calls to non-Younite users

External Link Have a meeting code?

Integration with popular conferencing applications: Younite seamlessly integrates with popular conferencing applications like Zoom, Teams, and GMeet. Join meetings as an invitee, bringing the powerful features of Younite to your existing conferencing workflow.

Dial In

Younite also provides the convenience of dialing cellular calls to contacts or numbers that don't have the Younite application. Stay connected with colleagues, friends, or clients, even if they are not Younite users.

Join QR

The QR simplifies the process of connecting and participating in conversations, events, and sessions. By leveraging QR code technology, users can quickly and securely join meetings, parties, or stage events with a seamless and hassle-free experience.

Chat

The in-app chat feature provides users with additional channels for communication, enabling real-time messaging, group collaborations, file sharing, multimedia support, and expressive interactions.

Party

Light & Bright. Full of life

Enjoy synchronized playback of songs and videos from local memory and YouTube, along with the option to add strobe lights for a captivating experience. Connect up to 256 participants for a truly immersive party.

Strobelights

Add an extra touch of excitement to your parties with strobe lights that sync with the music. Create an immersive atmosphere and enhance the visual experience for all participants.

Synchronized playback

Enjoy synchronized playback of songs and videos during parties and gatherings. Whether you're playing content from your local memory or streaming from YouTube, Younite ensures that everyone experiences the same audio and video simultaneously.

Support for large group

Connect up to 256 participants to a single party. Whether you're hosting a small gathering or a larger event, Younite ensures that everyone can join in the fun and entertainment.

Multi-Device Video Extension

Automatically detect and list available devices for easy connection. Support synchronized video playback across all connected devices. Extend the video display by swiping from one device to another
